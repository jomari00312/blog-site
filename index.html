What is CarbonROM?


CarbonROM is an aftermarket firmware based on the Android Open Source Project created with the purpose of adding versatility and customization to stock Android. Stability is our highest priority; our vision is to be the best alternative to a stock operating system for your device.
          I used CarbonROM for my daily driver because it is stable and smoother than other custom rom such as Pixel Experience, Lineage, Evolution X, Havoc, Stag, Corvus, Oxygen, Nitrogen, and many more. There are lot of custom rom that is more on customisation, tweaking, and good in performance but I prefer carbon because it has a nice interface but less in customisation. The reason why I use Carbon is because it is based on Google Pixel Devices that has built in nice camera and working properly in Carbon.üòÄ
